Account Deletion in FinTrack AI

In the FinTrack AI app, you can delete your account along with all associated data.

How to delete your account

  1. Open the FinTrack AI application.
  2. Go to Settings → Delete Account.
  3. Confirm the account deletion.

After confirming deletion:

  • Your account will be permanently deleted.
  • All personal data (email), transactions, categories, and settings will be removed from our servers with no possibility of recovery.
  • The deletion process is automatic and irreversible.
